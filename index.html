What Started as a Shop
Became a Legacy

Mithila Threads was never supposed to be a business. It was my grandfather's attempt to earn a living. Ranjit Kumar Ji opened a small shop in Bowbazar in 1987 with two handlooms and a reputation for honest dealing. He bought directly from weavers — never through middlemen — and passed the savings to customers.

Today we work with over 60 weaver families across four states. My mother, Shanti Devi, still selects every piece that enters the shop. She travels to Varanasi, Bengal, and Gujarat twice a year specifically for this. If she wouldn't wear it herself, it doesn't come to Bowbazar.
