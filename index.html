Est. 1987 — Bowbazar, Kolkata

Three Generations
of Weaving Dreams

Mithila Threads started as a tiny shop in Bowbazar in 1987, run by Ranjit Kumar Ji and his wife Shanti Devi. What started with two handlooms and a vision to bring authentic Bengali weaves to the common household has grown into a trusted name for ethnic wear across India.

Today, we work directly with over 50 weaver families in Varanasi, Bengal, and Gujarat — every piece sold means a livelihood preserved, a tradition kept alive.
