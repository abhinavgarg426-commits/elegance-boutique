Our Story

Crafted with Heart

Elegance & Co. was born in 2019 from a simple belief — Indian women deserve access to beautifully crafted, ethically made fashion without the luxury price tag.

Every piece in our collection is designed in Mumbai and crafted by a network of 40+ artisans across Rajasthan, Gujarat, and Maharashtra. We use natural fabrics — cotton, linen, silk, khadi — and traditional techniques like block printing, hand embroidery, and weaving that have been passed down through generations.

We're committed to sustainable, slow fashion. No mass production. No fast fashion shortcuts. Just quality pieces made to last and designed to make you feel extraordinary.
